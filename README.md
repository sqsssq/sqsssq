## Hi, I am Qing SHI (石青) 
My research interests center on designing human-centered interactive systems that use AI and visualization to support learning, reasoning, collaboration, and sensemaking while preserving human agency.

### Research Interests
- **AI for Education:** Interactive AI tools that scaffold critical thinking, reasoning, reflection, knowledge construction, and self-regulated learning.
- **HCI and Human-AI Collaboration:** Human-centered systems that support sensemaking, collaboration, decision-making, and agency in AI-assisted workflows.
- **Visualization and Visual Analytics:** Visual representations and interactive analytics systems for understanding complex cognitive, social, financial, and behavioral data.
